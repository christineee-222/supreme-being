Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project.

usage, modification, or redistribution requires explicit permission.

Contributions may be accepted on a case-by-case basis.

By submitting code, documentation, or ideas to this project, you agree:

* You are granting the project owner full rights to use, modify,
  and distribute your contribution as part of the project.
* You will not claim ownership of merged contributions.
* Contributions may be incorporated into commercial or
  proprietary versions of the software.
